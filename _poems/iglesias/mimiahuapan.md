---
title: Mimiahuapan
author: J.P. Iglesias
---

The rock-wall chokes
all sounds to mute, giving room
for the breeze to roar
as it grows to wind
and carries the song
of birds — sweet birds —
to the battle to unfold 
the clouds and let
the Sun that westers
paint with hues of rose and gold
the pillared faces
of the rock-wall.
