---
title: Litany of Dawn
author: J.P. Iglesias
---

Give glory to God <!--Athaolèir-->, starlit sky.
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Morning Star, bright herald:
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Praise Him.

Give glory to God <!--Athaolèir-->, birds of dawn.
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Feathered beasts, day's choir:
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Praise Him.

Wake us, Sun. From slumber: wake us.
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;May thy light hallow us.
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;May our works hallow Him.
