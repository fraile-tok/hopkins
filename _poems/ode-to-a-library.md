---
title: Ode to a Library
author: Regina Ochoa
---

Endless hallways and towering shelves 
Soft, velvet, colorful covers
What is it they hide behind their curtains? 
Delightful surprises await 
In the place where the stories never end
There is silence, but not the somber, deathly type
It's another kind of silence
One that encourages thoughts to fly far, far away
One that opens our souls to the heavens…
Delightful surprises await 
In the place where the stories never end 

Fairies dwell among the pages 
A pirate ship sails under a fire sky
Three dwarves hide in the corners
And a masked man watches from the shadows
You who seek adventure will find it here
Those who are hungry for love, for friends, for warmth
Seek no further, the destination is clear
Can you smell that? 
It's the best perfume in the world
That which scents those beautiful mystery pages 

Words….
Multicolored wings powerful enough to touch the stars
To travel in time and space
To talk to the dead and sing with the angels 
To discover icy planets and dive into endless oceans 
Words….
Our ticket to the realms of imagination
The only ones who understand our search for freedom
One who reads is one who lives a thousand lifetimes
A Library is Infinity under a roof
