Litany of Dawn

J.P. Iglesias

Give glory to God , starlit sky.

    Morning Star, bright herald:

            Praise Him.

Give glory to God , birds of dawn.

    Feathered beasts, day's choir:

            Praise Him.

Wake us, Sun. From slumber: wake us.

    May thy light hallow us.

        May our works hallow Him.
