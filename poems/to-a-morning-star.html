To a Morning Star

J.P. Iglesias

My Morning Star, I wish to say to you,

Elegant muse of yearnings deep to me,

Rarely my eyes have seen such beauty,

Clad in the most divine of earthly glows:

Ere the Last Dawn, my life's most just delight.

Do you conceive how deep you make me wish,

Each breath to be a gift I take for you?

Such grace must be our God's most perfect line.
